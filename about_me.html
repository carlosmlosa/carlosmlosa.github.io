About me

My story

I was born in Salamanca (Spain), 1999. My family early settled up in Madrid when I was a baby. Madrid was the place where I grew up. I went to a catholic school where I studied for 12 years. During that time I started playing soccer in my school where I rapidly standed out for my passion, dedication, compromise, leadership and team playing attitude. I was the team captain for more than 9 years, I have more than 10 national tournaments participations and I am 2016 senior league subchampion. I also combined my studies with the soccer team, english classes and karate (which I practice reaching out brown belt). The summers of 2015 and 2016 I spent a month living with a family in New Jersey and Chicago respectively, thank to that experiences I mastered my english and I got involved in the american culture. I finally, graduated with honors from High School and obtained an excellence scholarship for my grades in the university access exam (EVAU).

I started my university bachelor degree in 2017 in Telecommunication engineering at ETSIT UPM. In 2017 I also started coaching soccer in former categories at the school in which I used to play. I coached from ages 6 to 18 and I participated in 2 national tournaments. This experience help me to develope my communication skills, leadership, team-working, time management and plannification. During that time I also keep playing soccer in the Madrid's federation league, we became 2019-2020 league champions. In 2021 I graduated from my Bachelor degree in Telecommunication engineering with specialization in telematics. Obtaining a score of 10 out of 10 in my final bachelors thesis in which I designed and implemented a NLP tool for a cibersituational platform that was being developed in the RSTI group. The summer after graduating I participated with my colleges in the design, development and implementation of a software platform for a physical therapy clinic. We collaborate as freelance software developers for building the clinic managment platform.

After completing my bachelor degree, in 2021, I decided to continuing my formation pursuing a masters degree in Telecommunication engineering at ETSIT UPM. This master program was profesional oriented and it is focused on fulfiling profesional competences as project managment and working in projects with a large amount of people.

For the last year of my formation, I decided to move to Chicago and pursue a more specialized master degree in Artificial Intelligence at IIT, Illinois Institute of Technology. In this master I learned about Deep Learning, NLP, Online Social Networks, Data preparation and analysis, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. During the summer of 2023 I will be involved in a research project for developing a Computer Vision tool for "Animal Tracking and behaviour analyisis".
